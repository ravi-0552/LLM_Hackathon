Biography:
Dr. Meera Iyer focuses on extracting useful knowledge from large-scale datasets. Her research includes data mining, predictive analytics, educational data mining, and business intelligence. She has guided projects involving recommendation systems, customer analytics, smart city data platforms, and healthcare analytics. She encourages students to explore practical applications of machine learning using structured and unstructured datasets.
