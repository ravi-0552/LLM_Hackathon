Biography:
Dr. Priya Nair works in the fields of Internet of Things, Edge AI, and embedded intelligence. Her research combines low-power computing with artificial intelligence to build efficient smart systems. She supervises projects involving smart agriculture, intelligent transportation, environmental monitoring, and industrial automation using IoT devices and edge computing platforms. Students interested in hardware-integrated AI systems often select her as a project mentor.
