Biography:
Dr. Vikram Sharma specializes in Computer Vision and Deep Learning. His research explores intelligent image understanding, object detection, medical imaging, autonomous systems, and video analytics. He has supervised numerous projects involving smart surveillance, autonomous drones, healthcare imaging, and AI-powered vision applications. His laboratory focuses on applying convolutional neural networks, vision transformers, and modern deep learning architectures to solve real-world problems. Students interested in Computer Vision, Deep Learning, autonomous vehicles, robotics vision, or medical image analysis often work under his guidance. His research combines practical engineering with state-of-the-art AI techniques.
