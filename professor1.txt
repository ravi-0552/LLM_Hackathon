Biography:
Dr. Ananya Rao has over fourteen years of teaching and research experience in Artificial Intelligence and Natural Language Processing. Her primary research focuses on Large Language Models, Information Retrieval, Retrieval-Augmented Generation, multilingual AI systems, and semantic search. She actively supervises undergraduate and postgraduate projects related to conversational AI, intelligent tutoring systems, question answering, and educational technology. She has published more than sixty research papers in international conferences and journals. Her current work investigates how retrieval-based systems can improve the factual accuracy of language models. She encourages students to build practical AI applications using transformer models, embeddings, vector databases, and modern retrieval techniques. Students interested in chatbots, language models, search engines, semantic retrieval, or multilingual AI frequently choose her as a project supervisor because of her strong background in Natural Language Processing and Information Retrieval.
